Solly Stables

Our Mission Statement

*Solly Stables’ mission is to provide a unique equestrian experience, building self-confidence, fostering independence and caruing for horses by promoting compassion and learning through riding and educational experiences.

*Solly Stables strives to achieve this mission regardless of a person's ability to contribute financially.

*Through our 4H program, we want to reach out to kids and teenagers/adults. NO barn seems willing to have kids around these days…

*Helping someone who needs a new outlook experience the healing power of the horse spirit is secondary part of our mission, and the most powerful!! We view it as a community event; just being around horses lifts your spirits!!

Donations and Volunteering

If you would like to donate to Solly Stables we have several ways you can help.

Donate money for building improvements and maintenance by clicking on our gofundme link to the right on each page.

We are always in need of supplies such as toilet paper, paper towels, soap, trash bags, cat food…

All donations are tax deductible.

We are looking for volunteers for our programs and just general help around the barn while lessons are being held.

You would need to have a parent or guardian (if under 18)available for paperwork signing initially. And as long as you are 16 or older you don't need one to stay with you.

Boarding

We currently have several stalls open. $475 per month per stall.

Direct access to Fairmont Park with hours of beautiful trails.

ALL Boarders must follow the rules and regulations set by Solly Stables Riders Association, SSRA, and Fairmount Park. Including, but not limited to, deworming (we have both rotational and fecal programs), vaccinations, quarantine protocols, trail permits, equipment storage, property maintenance (i.e. cleaning up any manure dropped by your horse), and etc.....

*New horses must be in quarantine for 2 weeks.*
